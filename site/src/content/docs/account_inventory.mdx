Open from <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Toolbox", "Account Inventory"]} /> or <ToolboxPath kind="command" steps={["/show Account Inventory"]} />. Per-window options live under <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory"]} />.

The Account Inventory window shows a single combined view of every item you own — across all of your characters, their heroes, and your Xunlai chest — and remembers what it has seen so the items stay visible even when you are logged in on a different character. If you have several Guild Wars accounts registered with Toolbox, items from those accounts are shown too, marked as **foreign** so you can tell them apart.

## Header toolbar

The row of checkboxes along the top of the window controls how the items list is filtered and rendered. The same toggles are mirrored under <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory"]} />.

- **Detailed View** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Detailed View"]} /> — Switch between the compact icon grid (default) and a sortable table with `Character`, `Location / Hero`, `Model ID` and `Item` columns.
- **Merge Stacks** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Merge Stacks"]} /> — Combine multiple matching items into a single row, including normally non-stackable items. Hovering shows where each piece of the stack lives.
- **Hide other Accounts** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Hide other Accounts"]} /> — Hide items that belong to other GW accounts registered with Toolbox.
- **Hide Equipment** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Hide Equipment"]} /> — Hide items in the *Equipped Items* bag and any weapon-set entry currently marked equipped.
- **Hide Equipment Packs** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Hide Equipment Packs"]} /> — Hide everything inside the *Equipment Pack* bag.
- **Hide Hero Armor** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Hide Hero Armor"]} /> — Hide armor pieces worn by heroes (slots 2+ of a hero's equipped bag).
- **Hide unclaimed Items** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Hide unclaimed Items"]} /> — Hide the contents of the *Unclaimed Items* bag.
- **Gather Inventories** <ToolboxPath kind="window" steps={["Account Inventory", "Gather Inventories"]} /> — Walk through every character on the active account and every unlocked hero to refresh the saved inventory data. See [Gather Inventories](#gather-inventories) below.

## Free Slots

The collapsible **Free Slots** header expands into a sortable table showing how many inventory and equipment slots are free on each character and chest that Toolbox has seen, in the form `free/total`. Click a column header to sort; click again to flip the direction; hold `Shift` to sort by multiple columns.

Clicking the **character name** of an entry on the active account is a shortcut:

- On a player row — reroll to that character.
- On the `(Chest)` row — reroll/open the Xunlai chest on the active character at the first storage pane.

Entries from other accounts are shown greyed out. Chest rows from foreign accounts include the alphabetically-first character name on that account in square brackets (`(Chest) [SomeName]`) so you can distinguish multiple foreign chests without showing account email addresses.

The anniversary storage pane (Storage 14) is only counted toward the chest's max once Toolbox has seen at least one item in it.

## Items list

Below the toolbar sits the items list. The first row under the column headers is a per-column filter:

- **Character** filter — Substring match against the character name. Case-insensitive if your query is all lowercase.
- **Location / Hero** filter — Substring match against the bag/hero label (e.g. `Storage 3`, `Equipped Items`, `Olias`). Case-insensitive if all lowercase.
- **Model ID** filter — Exact match against the item's model id.
- **Item** filter — Substring match against the decoded item description. Case-insensitive if all lowercase.

To the right of the item filter, `Filter <shown>/<total> Items` shows how many items pass the current filter out of the total tracked.

The columns are also sort keys — click a header to sort by `Character`, `Location / Hero`, `Model ID` or `Item`; hold `Shift` to add secondary sort columns.

### Item colors

Each item button is tinted to tell you at a glance where it lives:

- **Default** — A normal inventory item on the active account.
- **Green-tinted** — Stored in the Xunlai chest (any storage pane) or material storage.
- **Yellow / orange-tinted** — Equipped on a hero.
- **Desaturated** — Belongs to another GW account registered with Toolbox.

The yellow number in the top-left of an icon (icon grid view) is the stack size.

### Clicking an item

Clicking an item is a "go to it" shortcut:

- **Click** — Reroll to the character that owns the item. If the item lives on a hero, that hero is added to the party. If the item is in the chest, Toolbox opens the Xunlai window and switches to the correct storage pane.
- **Ctrl+Click** — Same jump as above, *plus* automatically move the item between your inventory and the chest using the same logic as the Inventory Manager. Hero armor cannot be moved this way and is treated as a plain click. Items on other accounts cannot be moved either.

While a reroll/load is in progress, an `Account Inventory loading in progress` dialog appears with an **Abort!** button if you need to cancel.

Hovering an item shows a tooltip that lists each character/location the item is found on with per-location totals, plus the full item description. Items from foreign accounts are dimmed in the tooltip.

## Gather Inventories

By default, Toolbox only sees items on the character you are currently playing (plus your active heroes and chest). To populate the window with everything on the account, use **Gather Inventories**:

- Available both in the window's toolbar and under <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory", "Gather Inventories"]} />.
- Triggers a confirmation dialog before starting, because it will use the [Reroll](/docs/reroll/) feature to cycle through every PvE character on the account and read each one's inventory.
- On each character it kicks the party, adds every unlocked hero in batches of up to `max party size - 1` (capped at 7), waits for the hero's inventory to load, and records what is in their equipped bag.
- When finished it rerolls back to the character you started on and restores the heroes you had in your party.
- The whole pass can take several minutes if you have many characters/heroes. The progress dialog has an **Abort!** button.

The gather pass is skipped for PvP characters and for characters currently stuck on a map where Toolbox disables itself.

## Settings page

Under <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory"]} />:

- All seven toggles from the header toolbar (`Detailed View`, `Merge Stacks`, `Hide other Accounts`, `Hide Equipment`, `Hide Equipment Packs`, `Hide Hero Armor`, `Hide unclaimed Items`) with short descriptions next to each.
- **Gather Inventories** <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory", "Gather Inventories"]} /> — Same action as the in-window button.
- **Delete Account Inventory** <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory", "Delete Account Inventory"]} /> — Wipe the saved inventory data **for the active account only** and remove its `.ini` files from disk.
- **Delete All Inventories** <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Account Inventory", "Delete All Inventories"]} /> — Wipe saved inventory data for **every** account stored on disk, including foreign accounts.

After either delete action a reminder dialog notes that you should also disable **Account Inventory** under <ToolboxPath steps={["Settings", "Windows", "Account Inventory"]} /> if you don't want Toolbox to immediately start regathering data the next time you load a map.

## How the data is stored

Inventory data is persisted as `.ini` files under your Toolbox settings folder, in a sub-directory called `inventories`:

- One file per character holds that character's items.
- One file per account holds that account's Xunlai chest items.
- Files are saved on map load while you are in an **outpost**. Saves are suppressed in explorable areas so writes don't impact gameplay.
- When you log out, Toolbox writes out the current account's data and reloads any foreign-account data so it stays in sync between profiles.

You never need to edit these files by hand; this is documented so you know what gets touched if you back up or move your Toolbox settings folder.
