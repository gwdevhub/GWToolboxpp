The Danger Rings module draws hostile ground area-of-effect skills — Meteor Shower, Maelstrom, traps and similar — as rings on the ground in the 3D game world. The rings are composited under the in-game UI and depth-tested against the terrain, so they sit on the floor and are hidden by hills and walls like real geometry.

<p class="text-(--color-fg-meta)">This is a beta feature. If the in-world compositor fails to install, the settings panel says so at the top.</p>

## Settings

- **Occlude behind terrain** — hide the parts of a ring that fall behind hills, walls and other terrain. Off draws the whole ring on top.
- **Maximum render distance** — how far from the camera rings are drawn.
- **Ring thickness** — width of the ring band.
- **Rim opacity** / **Fill opacity** — how solid the ring's outline and its filled interior are (0 = invisible).
- **Height lift** — raises the rings slightly off the ground to avoid flicker (z-fighting) on flat terrain.
- **Pulse speed** — how fast the rings pulse, in Hz (0 = no pulsing).
