The Skill Range Rings module draws a hovered skill's ranges on the ground in the 3D game world. Hover any skill — on your skillbar, in the Skills window, in template previews — and its relevant ranges are draped onto the terrain:

- **Cast / use range** around you.
- **AoE radius** around your current target (or yourself).
- **Earshot** for shouts and chants.
- **Constant effect (spirit range)** where a skill has one.

The rings are composited under the in-game UI and depth-tested against the terrain.

<p class="text-(--color-fg-meta)">This is a beta feature. If the in-world compositor fails to install, the settings panel says so at the top.</p>

## Settings

- **Show AoE ring at your current target** — for targeted skills with an area effect, draw the AoE ring around your target instead of around you. Off always draws it around you.
- **Occlude behind terrain** — hide ring segments that fall behind terrain.
- **Maximum render distance** — how far from the camera rings are drawn.
- **Ring thickness** / **Opacity** — the ring band width and how solid it is.
- **Height lift** — raises rings slightly off the ground to avoid flicker (z-fighting).
- **Colours** — each range (AoE, earshot, constant effect) has its own colour.
